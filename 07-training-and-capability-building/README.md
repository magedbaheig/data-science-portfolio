## 💼 Business Problem & Objective

**Situation:** In many emerging analytics markets, there is a gap between academic data science theory and real-world commercial application—particularly in telecom and CX analytics contexts. Professionals often learn tools but struggle to translate outputs into business decisions.

**Task:** Design and deliver practical, industry-oriented, and end-to-end training programs that bridge this gap—equipping professionals with the analytical mindset and technical skills required to apply data science in real operational contexts.
